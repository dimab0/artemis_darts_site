Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

Artemis Darts (the “App”) is provided for entertainment and game-play tracking. This privacy policy explains how information is handled when you use the App on iOS.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not require account creation and we do not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.
  • Game Data: The App may store scores, match history, and gameplay preferences on your device so the App can function properly.
  • Diagnostics: Apple may provide anonymous crash or diagnostics data if you have enabled device analytics sharing in iOS settings.

How We Use Information

Any data used by the App is used only to:

  • Provide core app features (such as scorekeeping and saved game settings).
  • Maintain app stability and improve performance.

Data Storage

Gameplay data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a backend account system for Artemis Darts, and we do not sell or rent your data.

Third-Party Services

The App may rely on Apple-provided services (such as App Store distribution, optional diagnostics, or cloud backup features you enable on your device). Those services are governed by Apple's terms and privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Choices

  • You can delete app data by removing the App from your device.
  • You can manage analytics and diagnostic sharing in iOS Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
